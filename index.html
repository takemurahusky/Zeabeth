About Me

Meet Zeabeth, the world's most famous furry artist who was born as a humble furtilla (a tortilla with fur) in the cozy kitchen of Abuela's Taqueria. From the moment Zeabeth came off the griddle, it knew it was destined for greatness. Starting off with a simple life as a furtilla, Zeabeth dreamed of becoming a furrito (a furry burrito), renowned for its artistic flair. Through dedication and a dash of salsa, Zeabeth transformed, wrapping itself in a vibrant blanket of creativity. Now, as a full-fledged furrito, Zeabeth's artwork is celebrated worldwide, from taco stands to high-end galleries. Zeabeth's journey from furtilla to furrito is a spicy tale of passion, art, and just the right amount of guacamole.
